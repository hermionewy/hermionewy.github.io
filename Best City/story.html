America Ranks the First with 25 Cities Entered the Best Quality of Life List


What factors will make you happy when you move to a city?

NUMBEO, a website conducting research and providing data which are not influenced by any governmental organizations had released Quality of Life Index 2017 recently. The data provided scores of quality of life of major international cities. There are 177 cities which are mentioned in the dataset, 25 cities are in the US which makes US rank the first referring to the number of cities entered this year's chart, followed by Canada and India.
