Boston MV Accident Report

How scary is it to drive in Boston?


A Boston driver is 167.6% more likely to have an accident than the national average.

Massachusetts drivers were ranked the worst in the nation for the third year in a row, while drivers in Boston were named the worst of the worst.
According to the auto insurer Allstate’s annual “Best Drivers Report”, which ranks motorists in the 200 most populous cities in the country, a Boston driver has a new accident every 7.1 years on average. Fortunately, that’s up from every 3.9 years, estimated in last year’s report.
Boston has seen a significant rise on the number of motor vehicle accident reports of 2016, which is two times as large as the number of 2014.
